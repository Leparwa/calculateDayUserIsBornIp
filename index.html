Brief Description of this programm

Well, most people do know when their birthdays are, but some might not know what day of the week they were born. Luckily for us, we have found ways to determine the day of the week your were born. This web application takes your birthday and calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender i will tell you your Akan Name.
